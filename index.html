About

Oladimeji Aloba PK/A Mohbad (Imole) was a Nigerian rapper, singer, and songwriter from Lagos. He was formerly signed to Naira Marley's Marlian Records and left the label in 2022. He was best known for his hit singles "Ponmo, Feel Good", and "KPK (Ko Por Ke)" (You Will Always Be Remembered) with Rexxie which was nominated three times for The Headies awards 2022.
